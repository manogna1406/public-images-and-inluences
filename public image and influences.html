public image and influences

Arijit has shared his fondness for singer Kishore Kumar,[175] while citing Mohit Chauhan and Atif Aslam as inspirations with whom he is often compared.[176] Though he has been inspired by many artists, Singh named KK as his favourite singer along with Ayushmann Khurrana.[citation needed] He named Pritam Chakraborty, Vishal–Shekhar, Shankar–Ehsaan–Loy, Amit Trivedi, Mithoon and A. R. Rahman as his favourite music directors.[31] Singh has idolised Ghulam Ali, Jagjit Singh and Mehdi Hassan.[159] In an interview with Filmbeat in April 2013, he declared that he is the biggest fan of singer Krishnakumar Kunnath (KK).[citation needed] His desire to sing for Aamir Khan[175] came to reality in 2016 with the song "Naina" from Dangal.[177] In an interview with The Indian Express, Singh stated that he loves the work of Shalmali Kholgade, Shefali Alvares, and Irfan. He also mentioned that his "current favourite" singer is Chinmayi.[160] In 2016 he appeared on the popular chat and comedy show The Kapil Sharma Show aired on Sony TV India. The show's host Kapil Sharma explained the difficulties he had bringing Singh on the show due to his shy nature.
